# Romance

I like the idea of romance. Two people, each uncomplete, finding each other.

Many romance stories are actually Chosen One narratives instead, where the two people are very unequal in some way. Other stories that wear Romance's clothes are travelogues, sight-seeing tours of beautiful places where the romance is just to have a plot. Still other stories that pretend to be Romance are power fantasies, usually involving supernatural elements.

None of these are true romances, because none of these focus on ordinary, almost incomplete people who need to find their other halves.

If it's true that Mystery is the genre of plot, sci-fi the genre of setting, and fantasy the genre of theme, then Romance is the genre of character. Two people who change each other, improve each other, and become more than the sum of their individual worth.

## Adding Interactivity

If character change, especially via another character, is the important bit, then this might be the interactive bits and bobs.

If a character acts a certain unbest way, we drill down into that person's psyche to find the cause and effect of their life experiences and beliefs that lead to said behavior. We examine it, we question parts that don't make a lot of sense, we propose alternative viewpoints. And, finally, the character themself begins to experiment with new ways of thinking, of doing, of being, until it becomes habit, or at least validated.

So the landscape is one of beliefs ("should" statements), of impactful life stories (which is a chain of events that appear to be a solid cause-and-effect), and the connections between. The "weapons" of the other character are the questions of why, and are you sure, and what if, and simple denial. Of counter-stories and unconsidered possibilities.

And a character will fight for their wrong beliefs. Accusations, defensiveness, denial, straw-manning, disbelief. These are the "weapons" of defense. It isn't the character that is fighting a war of belief but the belief itself fighting for survival. The character is larger than their own one belief.

When closure is close, an "echo" of the other character can appear, can assist in crucial times of stress. But the original belief fights one last stand. It tries to trick the cause and effect to its own end. A steady hand wins the race. The final decision walks a new road.

## Pieces of You

A belief, a violation, an action, an effect. An effect to violate others beliefs. A chain of such, leading to an action, leading to a habit.

A question why, a rationalization, an alternative, a dismissal, a story, a crack in the edifice.

Thoughts become words become actions become habits become character become destiny.
